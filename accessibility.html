accessibility statement

This site was designed with sensitivity to the screen essential reader. That is to say that color is not necessarily a cue signify differentiated information but mainly for aesthetic purpose. The colors used in this site may be thought of as “inverted” from the norm; black text on white background. Some scientific evidence may prove that high contrast black text on white backgrounds may cause significant eye strain for the increasingly screen essentialised person. However, it is not yet scientifically proven. Using WAVE (Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool), i have checked each page of the site for errors and corrected each using WAVE's recommendations for repair.
if you'd like to, please contact me with any accessibility concerns, comments, or suggestions regarding this site.
